Imagine 12 years ago, when the most advanced mobile phones were those flip phones, the advanced ones with voice recognition and some of them even with color displays. I remember when I needed to write an essay for High School about technology...

We are living in "The Future"

Watch, Siri, home automation. Noach, machresha. The purpose of technology, but it doesn't work if we don't look over it.

Question your habits in today's World

  1. Wake up and check the phone
  2. Read/Send messages while talking to someone, and ignoring or not being able to understand what they say
  3. Are you adding something to people's lives when you share a photo on FB/Instagram? Have you ever thought if your friends want to see what you are eating for breakfast?
  4. Bragging
  5. Prayer concentration

What can wait Vs. What cannot

The subtle tap in your wrist

Never forget the priorities

It doesn't matter which platform, device, brand you choose. Just keep in mind why we use all this.
